Rose

The rose is one of the most iconic and beloved flowers in the world, admired for its exquisite beauty, fragrant aroma, and rich symbolism. Belonging to the Rosaceae family, the rose is a perennial plant with over 300 species and thousands of cultivars, each offering a unique combination of color, form, and fragrance.

Daisy

The daisy is a charming and cheerful flower that belongs to the Asteraceae family, one of the largest families of flowering plants. Known for its simplicity and beauty, the daisy is often associated with innocence, purity, and new beginnings.

Sunflower

The sunflower, scientifically known as Helianthus annuus, is a vibrant and iconic flower renowned for its striking appearance and its symbolic association with positivity and warmth. Belonging to the Asteraceae family, sunflowers are native to North America but have become beloved worldwide for their beauty and utility.

Dandelion

The dandelion, scientifically known as Taraxacum officinale, is a familiar and resilient flower that belongs to the Asteraceae family. Often considered a weed, the dandelion is, in fact, a versatile and valuable plant with a rich history of use in medicine, cuisine, and ecology.

Tulip

Tulips are a genus of spring-blooming perennial herbaceous bulbiferous geophytes. The flowers are usually large, showy and brightly coloured, generally red, pink, yellow, or white. They often have a different coloured blotch at the base of the tepals, internally.
